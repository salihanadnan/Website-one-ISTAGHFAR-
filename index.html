The Power of Istighfar

“And say, 'My Lord, forgive and have mercy, for You are the best of the merciful.'”
— Surah Al-Mu’minun (23:118)

No matter how heavy your sins feel, Allah’s mercy is greater. Istighfar opens the doors of rizq, peace, and blessings. Say: Astaghfirullah with sincerity—it can change your day and your life. Never underestimate the value of turning back to Allah.

Daily Action: Take 5 minutes today to sit quietly and say Astaghfirullah 100 times. Feel the mercy descend.
